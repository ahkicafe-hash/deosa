Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer private tastings?

Yes, we offer bespoke private tasting sessions for our couples. This allows you to experience our culinary presentation and curate your perfect menu before the big day.

How far in advance should we book?

Due to high demand during peak wedding seasons, we recommend securing your date 9 to 12 months in advance.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?

Absolutely. We pride ourselves on creating exceptional dishes for all guests, including bespoke vegan, gluten-free, and halal menus.
